Our Rooms

Deluxe Room

The Deluxe Room offers a perfect blend of luxury and comfort, designed to provide a relaxing escape from the everyday. This spacious room features a plush king-size bed, a cozy seating area, and a modern ensuite bathroom with a rain shower and premium toiletries. Enjoy stunning views from the large windows or private balcony, complemented by natural light. Amenities include high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and 24-hour room service, ensuring a seamless and indulgent stay. Ideal for couples and solo travelers seeking a refined retreat.

Price: $200/night

Suite

Experience the pinnacle of luxury in our elegantly designed Suite. Perfect for those seeking extra space and sophistication, the Suite features a separate living area and bedroom for added privacy and comfort. The bedroom boasts a plush king-size bed with premium linens, while the living area includes stylish furnishings, ideal for relaxation or entertaining. The lavish bathroom offers a soaking tub, a walk-in rain shower, and deluxe toiletries for a spa-like experience. Additional amenities include a private balcony with panoramic views, high-speed Wi-Fi, a large flat-screen TV, and a fully stocked minibar. The Suite promises an unforgettable stay, combining opulence and modern convenience.

Price: $300/night

Standard Room

The Standard Room provides a cozy and comfortable retreat, perfect for travelers seeking great value without compromising on quality. This well-appointed room features a queen-size bed with soft linens, a work desk, and a modern bathroom equipped with a walk-in shower and complimentary toiletries. Thoughtful amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and a coffee/tea maker ensure a convenient and pleasant stay. With its warm ambiance and functional design, the Standard Room is ideal for business travelers, couples, or solo adventurers.

Price: $120/night
